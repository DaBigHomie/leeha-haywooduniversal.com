## PHOTOGRAPHY GUIDELINES

### Image Style

**Characteristics:**
- Natural lighting (golden hour preferred)
- Warm color grading (+10 warmth, +5 saturation)
- Authentic moments (not overly posed)
- Atlanta locations visible when possible
- Diverse representation prominent
- Professional quality (sharp, well-composed)

### Subject Guidelines

**People Photography:**
- Eye level or slightly below
- Genuine smiles and expressions
- Professional attire (business casual to business formal)
- Diversity in age, skin tone, gender
- Success-oriented poses (confident, approachable)
- Natural environments (offices, properties, events)

**Property Photography:**
- Wide-angle for rooms (16-24mm)
- Vertical lines straight (no lens distortion)
- Well-lit (natural light + fill light)
- Decluttered and staged
- Hero shot + detail shots
- Dusk/twilight exterior shots

**Vehicle Photography:**
- Clean vehicles
- 3/4 front angle
- All angles (front, rear, sides, interior)
- Detail shots (dashboard, trunk, features)
- Against neutral or Atlanta backdrop

## RESPONSIVE DESIGN

### Mobile First Approach

**Priorities:**
1. Touch-friendly (44px minimum tap target)
2. Readable typography (16px minimum body)
3. Simplified navigation (hamburger menu)
4. Stackedlayouts
5. Performance optimized (<2s load time)

### Breakpoint Behavior

**< 640px (Mobile)**
- Single column layout
- Full-width cards
- Stacked forms
- Hamburger menu
- Large touch targets
- Bottom navigation (optional)

**640px - 1024px (Tablet)**
- Two column layout
- Sidebar transitions
- Grid layouts emerge
- Desktop navigation appears

**1024px+ (Desktop)**
- Multi-column layouts
- Sidebar persistent
- Hover states active
- Maximum information density

## ACCESSIBILITY STANDARDS

### WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance

**Color Contrast:**
- Body text: 4.5:1 minimum
- Large text: 3:1 minimum
- UI elements: 3:1 minimum

**Keyboard Navigation:**
- All interactive elements focusable
- Logical tab order
- Skip navigation links
- Focus indicators visible

**Screen Readers:**
- Semantic HTML
- ARIA labels where needed
- Alt text for images
- Form labels explicit

**Motion:**
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
- No auto-playing videos with sound
- Pauseable animations

## CONTENT VOICE & TONE

### Voice (Consistent)

**Characteristics:**
- Professional yet warm
- Confident yet humble
- Knowledgeable yet accessible
- Success-oriented yet realistic

**Language:**
- Active voice preferred
- Second person ("you", not "clients")
- Conversational (but not casual)
- Inclusive language
- Plain English (avoid jargon)

### Tone (Contextual)

**Homepage/Marketing:**
- Inspiring and aspirational
- Warm and welcoming
- Confident and professional

**Service Pages:**
- Clear and informative
- Benefits-focused
- Action-oriented

**Educational Content:**
- Helpful and empowering
- Patient and thorough
- Encouraging

**Legal/Technical:**
- Clear and transparent
- Professional and formal
- Reassuring
